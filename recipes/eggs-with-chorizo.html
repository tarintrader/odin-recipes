Eggs with Chorizo Recipe

Description

Huevos rancheros, also known as ranch-style eggs, are traditionally made with fried tortillas topped with sunny-side-up eggs covered in freshly made salsa. This is a variation of the traditional Mexican dish where we substitute the salsa for chorizo with pico de gallo; itâ€™s definitely the perfect Mexican brunch

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Chorizo
  • Potatoes
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Chop the chorizo to make it crumb
  2. Fried the eggs, add salt in the yellow
  3. Fried the potatoes
  4. Fried the crumbled chorizo
  5. On a dish put the fries as a base, top them with the chorizo and add the eggs.
