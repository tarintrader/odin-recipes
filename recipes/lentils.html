Lasagna Recipe

Description

This Colombian lentil recipe makes a great starter. You can make it with a bit more water and it will become somewhat of a soup, or you can let it cook and eat it with a plate of rice.

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 choped tomato
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Clean and cut the potatoes
  2. Add 1 portion of lentils with 3 of water in a pot
  3. Mix all the ingredients in the pot and cook in a medium fire for 45 minutes
  4. Lastly, correct salt if needed
