Lasagna Recipe

Description

Homemade lasagna isn't as hard to make as it seems. Don't be intimidated by all the layers of delicious Italian flavor — try this homemade lasagna recipe today!

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Sliced meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cooking cream
  • Grating cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook the pasta
  2. Cook the meat in a pan
  3. Create the lasagne structure alternating pasta, meat, tomato sauce and cream
  4. Lastly, add grating cheese on the top
  5. Bake for 20 minutes in an oven at 180ºC
